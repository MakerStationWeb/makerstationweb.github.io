About the Maker Station

The Maker Station provides students with access to digital fabrication technologies that bridge traditional art practices with cutting-edge creative technology. Our comprehensive resource libraries help students at all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners.

Accessible Learning

Resources specifically curated for art students, prioritizing creative expression over technical complexity. No prior engineering knowledge required.

Free & Open Tools

Emphasis on free, web-based, and educational software to ensure all students can access professional-quality design tools.

Creative Focus

Tools and tutorials selected for artistic applications across jewelry, fashion, sculpture, product design, and more.
