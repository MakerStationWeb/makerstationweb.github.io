Equipment Access

Access to Maker Station equipment is available for students working on creative projects. Equipment training and supervision may be required for certain tools.

How to Access Equipment

  1. Contact the Maker Station team to discuss your project needs
  2. Complete any required equipment training or inductions
  3. Book equipment time through the SISO system
  4. Bring your project files and materials to your session
  5. Follow all safety protocols and equipment guidelines
